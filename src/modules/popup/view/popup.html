Simple Dual Subtitles

Extension to view two sets of subtitles at once on Viki.com

How to Use:

  1. Open a viki episode or movie.
  2. Switch subtitles to the target language and back.
  3. Open the extension and select the desired target language.

A select box will appear in this popup as soon as any subtitles are loaded. It will update, if you load new ones on Viki.
